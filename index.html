About Me

I'm a creative and motivated developer currently studying at Eötvös Loránd University (ELTE), passionate about combining logical problem-solving with clean, aesthetic design. I enjoy transforming ideas into interactive digital experiences through code. Whether it’s building games, crafting websites, or automating systems, I thrive in learning environments and collaborative projects. My curiosity pushes me to explore new technologies, improve existing skills, and develop tools that are not only functional but enjoyable to use.

Academics

I am pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at ELTE, where I have gained solid theoretical foundations in algorithms, data structures, software engineering, and system design. My academic journey has also included practical projects in object-oriented programming, web development, and game logic implementation. Through both solo and team-based coursework, I’ve become proficient in languages like Python, Java, and JavaScript, and have worked with tools such as Git, VS Code, and Linux.
